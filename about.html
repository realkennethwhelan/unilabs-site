About UniLabs

A trusted brand that delivers quality every time

Based in Dublin, Ireland, UniLabs is a representative agency specialising in photonics — connecting customers with the world's leading laser, detector, and transducer manufacturers.

Our Story

Who we are

UniLabs was founded with a clear mission: to bring the highest quality photonics technology to customers in Ireland and beyond, backed by genuine technical expertise and responsive local support.

As an authorised representative agency, we act as the direct point of contact for a select group of globally recognised photonics manufacturers. This means our customers get the best of both worlds — world-class products and a dedicated local team that understands their needs.

Our team consists of experienced professionals with backgrounds in optical physics, photonics engineering, and technical sales. We don't just sell products — we understand them, and we work alongside our customers to ensure every solution we provide truly fits their application.

Our Journey

How we got here

2022

UniLabs Founded

UniLabs was established in Dublin, Ireland, with a focus on bringing precision photonics technology to the Irish and European market.

2023

First Partner Agreements

Signed representative agreements with Alpes Lasers, establishing our first product portfolio in mid-IR quantum cascade lasers.

2024

Portfolio Expansion

Added Sacher Lasertechnik and Keller Pressure to our partner network, broadening our offering to include tunable laser systems and precision transducers.

2025

Growing Team & Services

Expanded our consultation and optical engineering services, deepening our technical capability and building lasting relationships with research and industrial customers.
